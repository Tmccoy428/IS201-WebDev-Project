My Experience Trying to Build a Scheduling App

In the following I will describe my efforts to build a scheduling app with AI, how it failed, and what I decided to do instead.

  1. The Idea
    • I needed to create a web app using AI
    • I had previously wanted to learn how to do just that to build a scheduling app rather than using google sheets
    • I therefore decided that this would be my goal
  2. The Attempt
    • I asked AI to make it for me descibing its basic needs and functions
    • It made it pretty well for the amount of time it took, I imagined that the fine tuning would be light work
    • It wasn't
  3. The Defeat
    • I tinkered with it for hours trying to get it to understand what I was asking for
    • It took a while, but it became about 90% funcional
    • The last ten percent seemed impossible; I kept telling it to change something, but it never seemed to work
    • I gave up deciding to create something different
  4. The Formulating of a New Idea
    • I decided to take a break and focus on my scratch page assignment that is part of the same project
    • I wrote of my defeat at the hands of the scheduling app while trying to figure out what I should do instead
    • Eureka! I typed hoping it would jump-start the creative juices in my brain
    • I decided to work on the CSS for the scratch page while I thought about what I would do for my AI app
  5. The Idea is Formed
    • I decided to make a 'hard to use calculator'
    • The idea came to me because I was irritated at technology and thought it would be funny if my app was intentionally irritating
    • Enjoy the calculator
